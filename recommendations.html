My Recommendations

A curated catalog of the music, films, and cultural moments that have shaped my worldview and continue to inspire me.

From groundbreaking albums to thought-provoking cinema, these are the creative works and cultural touchstones that resonate with my values of social justice, environmental consciousness, and human connection.

Music That Moves Me

Phoebe Bridgers - Stranger in the Alps

Indie Folk / Singer-Songwriter

Raw vulnerability meets exquisite songcraft. Bridgers' debut captures the complexity of mental health, relationships, and finding your place in the world with unflinching honesty.

Frank Ocean - Blonde

R&B / Alternative Hip-Hop

A masterpiece of queer artistry and emotional depth. Ocean's exploration of love, identity, and masculinity broke new ground and continues to resonate deeply.

Mitski - Be the Cowboy

Art Pop / Indie Rock

Precision-crafted pop songs that tackle loneliness, ambition, and the Asian American experience with devastating clarity and innovative soundscapes.

Moon by Moon

Indie Folk / Local Maryland

A young Maryland band creating ethereal, introspective music that captures the beauty of intimate storytelling and atmospheric soundscapes. Their thoughtful approach to songcraft reflects a deep connection to place and community.

Cinema

These are my Letterboxd Top 4 - films that have profoundly shaped my perspective and continue to inspire me.

Zola (2020)

Dark Comedy / Crime

Janicza Bravo's wild adaptation of the viral Twitter thread. A surreal, darkly funny journey that captures the chaos of social media storytelling and female friendship gone wrong.

Clue (1985)

Mystery Comedy / Cult Classic

Jonathan Lynn's brilliant board game adaptation with rapid-fire dialogue and perfect comic timing. A murder mystery that's endlessly rewatchable, with every actor delivering iconic performances.

Drive-Away Dolls (2024)

Queer Crime Comedy / Road Trip

Ethan Coen's raucous lesbian road trip comedy filled with stolen briefcases, dildo sculptures, and pure chaotic energy. A celebration of queer joy and female friendship with pitch-perfect absurdist humor.

Bottoms (2023)

Teen Comedy / LGBTQ+

Emma Seligman's hilariously unhinged high school satire about two queer students starting a fight club. Sharp, subversive, and absolutely committed to its own ridiculous premise.

Essential Reading

Parable of the Sower

Octavia Butler's prescient dystopian novel about climate change, community building, and adaptive change. Essential reading for our current moment.

Parable of the Sower

Her Body and Other Parties

Carmen Maria Machado's stunning collection of stories about women's bodies, desire, and horror. Boundary-pushing feminist fiction at its finest.

adrienne maree brown's revolutionary approach to organizing and social change, drawing inspiration from nature and science fiction.
